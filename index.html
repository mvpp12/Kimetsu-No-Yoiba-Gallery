Kimetsu No Yaiba

Kimetsu no Yaiba (Demon Slayer) is a Japanese anime and manga series that follows the journey of Tanjiro Kamado, a kind-hearted boy whose family is slaughtered by demons. His younger sister, Nezuko, survives but is transformed into a demon, setting Tanjiro on a mission to cure her and avenge their loved ones.
